About MindMesh

Our mission is to make mental health support accessible, stigma-free, and empowering for students everywhere.

Our Vision

We believe that every student deserves a safe space to express themselves, access professional help, and grow emotionally stronger. MindMesh bridges the gap between peer support, professional counsellors, and AI-driven wellness tools.

AI-Guided Support

24/7 AI chatbot providing immediate coping strategies and crisis intervention with seamless handoff to human counsellors.

Confidential Booking

Secure, anonymous appointment system connecting you with licensed counsellors and mental health professionals.

Peer Community

Moderated peer-to-peer support forums with trained student volunteers creating safe spaces for sharing.

Wellness Resources

Comprehensive library of mental health resources, relaxation audio, and wellness guides in multiple languages.

Smart Analytics

Anonymous data insights for institutions to identify trends and implement targeted mental health interventions.

Crisis Support

Immediate crisis intervention protocols with direct connection to emergency mental health services and helplines.
